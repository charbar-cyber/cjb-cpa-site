Background

I earned both my bachelor's and master's degrees in accounting from the University of Georgia, with a focus on taxation. My career in public accounting spans tax compliance, tax strategy, and audit, accounting, and advisory work for individuals, businesses, governments, and not-for-profits.

I began in a traditional CPA firm environment, working across a broad range of clients, and later expanded into more strategy-focused and technology-forward roles. My work today emphasizes clean execution, proactive planning, and clear communication.
