Pricing

What it costs to work with me.

Every engagement gets a written fee before any work starts. The figures here are anchor points for common situations — your actual fee depends on the scope we agree on together.

How billing works

Tax returns and other defined-scope work are quoted as flat fees. A retainer is collected at signing (typically $500 for returns, more for larger engagements), with the balance invoiced at delivery. Hourly work is billed monthly. If the scope shifts midstream, I'll flag it before any additional fee is incurred.

02 — Fractional CFO

CFO support on a monthly retainer.

For companies that need executive-level financial leadership without the full-time hire. Each tier reflects a different reporting cadence and depth of analysis. All retainers are month-to-month and can be paused or upgraded with 30 days' notice.

Foundation
$2,500/mo

Right when you want monthly financial visibility and a sounding board, without standing up an internal finance function.

  • Monthly close review and sign-off
  • One monthly working session
  • Quarterly KPI snapshot
  • Email and Slack access during business hours
Operating
$5,000/mo

For teams making real cash, hiring, and pricing decisions where the wrong call costs real money.

  • Everything in Foundation
  • Monthly KPI and financial review
  • Two standing working sessions per month

  • Budget vs. actual with commentary
Executive
